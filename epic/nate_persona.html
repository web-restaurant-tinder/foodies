Persona

Name: Julian Ortiz

Age: 23

Profession: Student

Technology: Windows user. Uses a HP Envy with Intel Core i5 (prefers chrome), a Surface Pro, and a Samsung S10 with unlimited 4G data connection/

Attitudes and Behaviors: Julian is a student at the University of New Mexico. He is majoring in computer science. Julian likes to hang out with friends and his girlfriend when not in class or doing homework. His hobbies include going out with friends, skiing, cooking, and doing activities with his girlfriend. Being a full-time student, Julian, is always on the go and values extra time he has to relax and unwind.

Goals and Needs: Julian wants a way to discover new restaurants and a way to quickly agree on a place to eat when trying to decide where to dine with his friends and/or girlfriend.

Frustrations:Not being able to agree on a place to eat.
Unable to find new restaurants in an easy way

User Story: As a user, I want an app where I can discover new restaurants and helps me find a restaurant that me and my friends/girlfriend agree on.

Interaction Flow:
User opens Play Store app on his Samsung Galaxy.
User searches for the Foodies app.
User downloads the app.
User opens the app.
User signs up with his email, username, phone number, and password.
User uploads picture.
User goes to his Gmail account and confirms email.
User signs in.
User sees a picture of a restaurant with an option to swipe right for like and left for dislike.
User swipes left on the first restaurant that he did not like.
The app stores the swipe and will not show it again.
User swipes left on the second restaurant that he liked.
The app stores the swipe and adds the restaurant to his likes.
User searches for girlfriend with the search bar.
The app brings up her page
User clicks follow to follow her.
User gets a notification telling him he has a new follower.
User navigates to the followers page and sees its his girlfriend.
User sees his matches with his girlfriend.
User logs out.
